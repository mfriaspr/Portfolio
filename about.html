About Me

Hi, I'm Maricarmen Frias Prieto, currently based in Davis, CA. I grew up in Salinas, CA, a vibrant community predominantly composed of Hispanic immigrant families. As the first member of my family to embark on and successfully complete a college journey, I'm deeply proud to have graduated from the University of California, Davis in the summer of 2023, earning a degree in Design with a minor in computer science.

Driven by a passion for innovation and user-centric design, I aspire to become a front-end developer or a UI/UX Designer. My journey has been enriched by invaluable experiences, including immersive internships in UI/UX Design. These opportunities gave me the chance to collaborate closely with multidisciplinary teams comprising engineers, product managers, and fellow designers. Through these collaborations, I honed my ability to offer and receive constructive criticism, leveraging it as an opportunity for growth and innovation.

During my free time, you'll often find me immersed in the exciting realm of app development. Currently, I'm working on a project close to my heart—Homely—an innovative app tailored for college housemates to streamline communication regarding chores and grocery shopping. Engaged in rigorous research and forming the competitive analysis, I'm laying the groundwork, eagerly anticipating the creation of wireframes that will shape its user experience. Ultimately, my vision is to translate this concept into reality by coding the app using React.

When I am not designing or coding, I find joy in exploring a wide range of interests. Thrift shopping serves as both a creative outlet and a source of inspiration, fueling my love for uncovering unique affordable treasures. Additionally, I love spending time outdoors whether that's playing volleyball or going on a summer hike.

I’m a passionate advocate for innovation and committed to leveraging design to cause meaningful change. I’m excited to start new adventures and embrace whatever challenges may come.
